@LATINASINBUSINESS

I am starting a new project to reach out to hundreds of young women starting off in business. The concept is for them to see examples of Latina women who have broken barriers. I plan on highlighting a different woman every week to share her accomplishments and her story. I would like to reach out to these women and ask if they could take over the page for a week to give the followers an insight to their work week, share favorite quotes, career, and life tips. I would like to ask them to share a bit of their personal life to show balance.

The idea came to me as I was scrolling through Instagram. I want to follow better content, to see content that is beneficial to my career and provides encouragement. So, I created an Instagram page called Latinas in Business.
As I learn to code in my web design class I am creating a page to go hand-in-hand with the Instagram page. The purpose of the website is to provide additional resources. With several poor leadership examples in the web today
